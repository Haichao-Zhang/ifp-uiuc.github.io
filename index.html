About Our Group

The IFP Group is founded by Professor Thomas S. Huang, started as Image Formation and Processing Group at Beckman Institute for Advanced Science and Technology from the late 80s. Over the years, the IFP Group has pursued research and innovation beyond images, inlcuding Image and Video Coding, Multimodal Human Computer Interaction, Multimedia Annotation and Search, Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition, Big Data, Deep Learning and High Performance Computing.

We are currently looking for a few talented and motivated PhD students and visiting scholars, please send your CV to us if you are interested.
